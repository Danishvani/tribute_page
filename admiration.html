Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. Known for his aggressive style of play and exceptional batting skills, Kohli has redefined modern cricket. He is one of the world's top batsmen, with numerous records in international cricket.

Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, Kohli's journey to becoming a cricket icon started early. He made his debut for the Indian team in 2008 and has since been a cornerstone of the squad. Kohli's leadership, work ethic, and passion have earned him respect worldwide.

He is not only a cricketing legend but also a role model for millions. Beyond cricket, he advocates fitness and runs a charitable foundation focusing on underprivileged children.
